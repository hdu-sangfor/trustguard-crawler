### 页面正文
In order to carry out its tasks, the Agency has a budget of 25.2 million euros for the year 2023. The funding of the ENISA consists of a contribution from the EU Budget, rent subsidy from the Government of the Hellenic Republic, as well as contributions from third countries participating in the work of the Agency.
The expenditure of the Agency includes staff (Title 1), administrative and infrastructure (Title 2), as well as the operational expenditure (Title 3), linked to the Agency's activities. The Executive Director implements the Agency's budget.
