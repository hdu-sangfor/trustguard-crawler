### 页面正文
Building on ENISA’s mission for a trusted and cyber-secure Europe, the Agency runs awareness-raising campaigns to promote good cybersecurity practices and foster behavioural and cultural change. With a comprehensive portfolio, ENISA organises a large range of thematic campaigns in collaboration with public and private sector stakeholders, who act as multipliers to ensure greater outreach.
Through these campaigns, ENISA not only disseminates and promotes its work in specific cybersecurity fields but also educates stakeholders and the public on regulatory developments and the latest technical advancements. A key focus of these efforts is improving cyber hygiene, empowering individuals and organisations with the knowledge and habits needed to identify and mitigate everyday cyber risks.
ENISA’s awareness activities also support larger causes, ultimately aiming to create a diverse, resilient, and cyber-aware ecosystem where good cyber hygiene is embedded as a standard practice across all sectors.
