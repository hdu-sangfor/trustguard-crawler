2025 APT Group Research Annual Report
The APT Group Research Annual Report 2025 is built on knowledge graphs and intelligent threat analysis, featuring an intelligence archive housing 662 APT group profiles. This report delivers detailed intelligence portraits for the 42 newly identified APT groups in 2025, alongside in-depth analysis of updated tactics, techniques and procedures (TTPs).
This report aims to present the latest research findings on APT groups and contribute to the continuous improvement of cybersecurity defense capabilities. Our objective is to move from the unknown to the known, from visibility to comprehensive monitoring, and from monitoring to full-scale response and mitigation, thereby neutralizing APT threats at their earliest stages. By increasing threat groups’ operational costs, strengthening deterrence, enhancing cyber resilience, and improving rapid response capabilities, we strive to contribute to maintaining security and stability of global society in cyberspace.
