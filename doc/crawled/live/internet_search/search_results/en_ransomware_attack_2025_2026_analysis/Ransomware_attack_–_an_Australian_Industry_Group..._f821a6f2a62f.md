Join us for the first in a new series of Australian Industry Group Online Events focussed on supporting our members in the increasingly crucial area of cyber security.
Four years ago, Queensland-based Australian Industry Group member B&R Enclosures was the victim of a significant ransomware attack.
In this online event, the company's Director, Chris Bridges-Taylor, joins Australian Industry Group's Cyber Consultant, Mark Schmidt, to discuss her experience, the unexpected business impacts, and her learnings from that traumatic event.
Importantly, this interactive Q&A session unpacked the key take-aways for SMEs and provide practical tips on how organisations can protect themselves from this continuously evolving threat.
