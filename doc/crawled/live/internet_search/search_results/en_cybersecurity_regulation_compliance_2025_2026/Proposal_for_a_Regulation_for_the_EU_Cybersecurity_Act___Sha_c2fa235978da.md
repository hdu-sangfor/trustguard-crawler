### 页面正文
The Proposal for a revised Cybersecurity Act is part of this package. It aims to increase cybersecurity capabilities and resilience and prevent fragmentation across the EU digital single market. It also enhances the security of the EU's Information and Communication Technologies (ICT) supply chains. It ensures that products reaching EU citizens are cyber-secure by design through a simpler certification process. It also facilitates compliance with existing EU cybersecurity rules and reinforces the EU Agency for Cybersecurity (ENISA) in supporting Member States and the EU in managing cybersecurity threats.
