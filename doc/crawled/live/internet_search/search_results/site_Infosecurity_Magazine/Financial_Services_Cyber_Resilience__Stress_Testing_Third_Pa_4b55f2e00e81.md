### 页面正文
Financial services firms are moving beyond simply ticking compliance boxes. Today, the focus is on stress testing digital supply chains, simulating cyber-attacks and uncovering hidden vulnerabilities to stay ahead of threats.
Regulatory pressure is accelerating this change, but compliance alone won’t deliver resilience. Many organizations still struggle with fragmented visibility across suppliers, poor coordination between teams, and security testing that fails to reflect real business‑impacting scenarios.
Strengthening third‑party cybersecurity and operational resilience is no longer optional. It’s critical. Join this webinar to learn why this shift matters now, and what firms must do next.
Register for this session to learn:
- How organizations move from mapping third parties to understanding real business impact
- Why paper-based assurance is no longer fit for purpose and how organizations can test controls under realistic stress conditions
- How regulation is reshaping expectations
- Where Financial Services firms are still falling short, and the critical gaps attackers and regulators alike continue to expose
