### 页面正文
According to the latest MetaCompliance research, 68% of CISOs say employees are their organisation’s biggest cyber security threat. Yet many organisations still lack the frameworks needed to meaningfully support their people and manage human cyber risk effectively.
Despite increased investments in security awareness training, many initiatives are designed to demonstrate compliance and coverage rather than drive lasting behavioural change. At the same time, responsibility for human-led incidents remains siloed within security teams, rather than being embedded across the organisation.
In this webinar, we'll explore how leaders can move beyond “weakest-link thinking” and instead, treat human cyber risk as a measurable, manageable discipline, much like technical risk.
Join this session to learn:
- How to frame human cyber risk as a structured risk management challenge that can be measured and improved
- Why traditional awareness programs fall short and what it takes to drive real behavioural change across the workforce
- How to embed shared accountability by aligning leadership, security teams and employees around a more effective human risk strategy
