### 页面正文
Cyber risk is accelerating and the nature of the threat is evolving. Recent developments in frontier AI models are beginning to increase the speed and scale of cyber-attacks, reinforcing what many organizations are already experiencing: incidents are becoming harder to predict, faster to unfold and more complex to manage.
At the same time, many organizations still manage cyber risk across fragmented tools, spreadsheets, and disconnected processes. When incidents occur, leaders are left asking critical questions: what’s impacted, who owns it and what evidence can we show right now?
Join this session to learn:
- How rising cyber threats, including those powered by AI, expose the limits of fragmented cyber programs
- Why cyber resilience is now a test of operational readiness and governance
- How to build connected, defensible cyber resilience that stands up under pressure
