### 页面正文
IT and security leaders are now expected to explain AI-driven changes in security at board level.
AI is no longer a future concern, it's already embedded across businesses processes and decision-making. With boards pushing for increased and rapid AI adoption, IT and security teams are walking a tightrope to adopt quickly and provide fast and clear answers on the risk, controls and measurable impact.
Join experts from LRQA and Thales as they discuss insights from LRQA’s The Defender’s Window research and the Thales 2026 Bad Bot Report.
The session will explore how AI is transforming the cybersecurity landscape, the challenges this creates for organizations, and the practical steps IT leaders can take to manage risk while enabling business value.
Join this session to learn:
- What fundamental changes frontier AI, like Mythos, is bringing to the cybersecurity landscape and how to keep up with the pace of change
- How to tackle the surge in AI-driven bott attacks as AI blurs the lines between legitimate and malicious activity (pulled from Thales report)
- How IT and security teams can enable innovations by balancing AI adoption with safety and security governance
- The opportunities frontier AI presents to defenders and best practices for use
